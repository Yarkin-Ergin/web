How do we understand what sentences mean? How much do syntax, prosody, and context matter?

What are the cognitive mechanisms that make some sentences easier to process, and some harder?

How do we apply quantitative methods to answer these? Why does statistics work?

These questions govern my research as a PhD candidate at Rutgers University.
