How do we understand what sentences mean? How much do syntax, prosody, and context matter?

What are the cognitive mechanisms that make some sentences easier to process, and some harder?

How do we apply quantitative methods to answer these? Why do statistics work?

These are the types of questions that occupied my mind in my research. I am a cognitive scientist with experience in psycholinguistics with a focus on sentence processing. For my research, I mastered a variety of behavioral, computational, and statistical skills to conduct my experiments, results of which have been presented at over 10 domestic and international conferences.
